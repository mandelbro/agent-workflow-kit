# Retrospective Workflow

## Purpose

Conduct a structured retrospective after completing a sprint or significant milestone.

## Process

1. **Gather Data** — Review completed tasks, velocity, and incidents
2. **What Went Well** — Identify practices to continue
3. **What Could Improve** — Identify pain points and friction
4. **Action Items** — Define concrete improvements for next sprint
5. **Document** — Save retrospective notes in sprint record

## Output

Save retrospective to `docs/project-management/sprints/Sprint-YYYY-MM-DD..YYYY-MM-DD.md` in the Retrospective Notes section.
